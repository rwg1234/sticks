About

Creators: Maximilian Donar, Rory Whyms Graham, Yiwei Liu and Lewis Munro.

The application on this webpage is intended to be used to help to teach children understand the notion of "the base" - grouping elements with different bases (base 2, base 10, ...). It has been developed for the School of Education of the University of Glasgow as a part of a Team Project for the Computing Science course at the University of Glasgow.
